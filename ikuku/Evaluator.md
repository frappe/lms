The ikuku section of the Frappe LMS is co-created with Kiro. 
The scripts and installers are AI generated( and set to be maintained by AI),
if there is a humanized version of the documentation it will be easier for the Human In the Loop.

To this effect, a webcomics featuring the intented use of ikuku by the intented Persona will serve the purpose.

The persona is Eva Lua Tour - a fairly tech savvy busy lady in an educational institution with a teaching main role and an additional role of interfacing with various IT( internal as well as external) providers.

She has a small team of volunteer co-workers to assist her in her initiatives.

Her institution is primarily Windows based and there is Linux servers and cloud access for specific functionalities.

Previously, without ikuku she would simply skip evaluating Frappe LMS even as she has heared good reviews about it simply due to the friction involved in provisioning and accessing Linux boxes. 

When someone says 'Use Docker' - things work the same irrespective of underlying platform, she typically rolls her eyes and says 'Oh, Yeah. Indeed' and mentally recollects how Docker desktop for Windows is powered by WSL( 1, 2, is there a 3 yet?) and its quirks - for example the dockerized software goes off on logout.
And there is also the Docker License to worry about - is their educational institution expected to pay for Docker Desktop. Again mentally recollecting - their internal IT departmen hates answering License questions. Understandably...

With ikuku, it is possible to do the initial smoke test after the install. And delegate more detailed specific feature evaluation to her collegue. Who inturn collaborates with another to do a side by side comparision with another LMS.

They typically revisit the features after comparing their notes. And consolidate the evaluation over 2-3 weeks period. 
And in the fourth week, create a fresh demo install to substantiate their evaluation results with working software. So that it is easy for the purchase/development/IT teams to progress on their original objective.

Making ikuku least mystical is the overall objective of the webcomic.
